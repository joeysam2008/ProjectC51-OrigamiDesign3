IMPORTANCE OF ORIGAMI

  • Origami excites other modalities of learning.
  • It has been shown to improve spatial visualization skills using hands-on learning. Such skills allow children to comprehend, characterize, and construct their own vernacular for the world around them.
  • It develops eye hand co-ordination, sequencing skills, spatial skills, memory, but also patience and attention skills
  • Origami can improve your maths too!
  • Origami can benefit your brain and mind! You also can learn to be patient.
